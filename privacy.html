Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026

Aimless (listed as "Aimless Radio" on the App Store) is a free radio streaming app. It does not collect your data. This policy explains what happens (and what doesn't) when you use the app.

What we collect

Nothing. Aimless does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-ins, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

What stays on your device

Your listening history, saved songs, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone. If you delete the app, it's gone.

Radio streams

Aimless connects directly to publicly available radio stream URLs to play audio. These connections are between your device and the station's server. We don't proxy, log, or monitor your listening.

Third-party services

Aimless uses the following Apple and third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple ShazamKit — identifies songs from audio. Processed through Apple's services. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple MusicKit — lets you save songs to Apple Music. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Spotify SDK — lets you save songs to Spotify. When you authenticate with Spotify, their privacy policy applies. See Spotify's Privacy Policy.

These integrations are optional. Aimless works without them.

Data sharing and selling

We don't share data because we don't have any. We don't sell data because we don't have any.

Children's privacy

Aimless does not collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
